**HBBU Arcade | Hair B&B University**

## Game Title
Bleach It or Botch It!

## Inspired By
https://mirellamanelli.com/best-hair-bleach-on-dark-hair-ranked-in-2025/
Mirella Manelli's ranked guide to the top 10 professional hair bleaches for dark hair in 2025,

## Game Genre
Trivia / Quiz — Jeopardy-style board game

## Concept Summary
Players step into the role of a salon floor contestant being quizzed on their professional
knowledge of hair bleach products, lightening science, application technique, and brand trivia —
all drawn directly from Mirella Manelli's expert bleach ranking guide. Right answers build your
score and stylist rank; wrong answers cost you dollars (and pride). The Daily Double mechanic
keeps every round unpredictable.

---

## Gameplay Mechanics
- Classic Jeopardy board: 5 categories x 5 clues = 25 total questions
- Dollar values increase with difficulty: $200 / $400 / $600 / $800 / $1,000
- Player clicks a dollar amount, reads the clue, and types their answer
- Answers are accepted in "What is ___?" form OR as plain typed responses
- Fuzzy matching: partial correct answers are accepted (e.g., "hyaluronic" scores full credit)
- Correct answer: adds full dollar value to score; shows green popup confirmation
- Wrong answer: deducts dollar value; reveals correct answer immediately
- Skip/Pass option: skips the question with no deduction
- 3 hidden Daily Double tiles: double the point value — revealed by a full-screen animation

## Categories (5)
1. **Brand Breakdown** — Identify bleach brands from descriptions of their formulas and results
2. **The Manelli Scale** — Questions about Mirella's proprietary 10-category scoring system
3. **Lift & Science** — Hair chemistry: lift levels, ingredients, bond builders, chemical behavior
4. **Technique Talk** — Application methods: balayage, foil work, processing time, mixing tools
5. **Stylist Smarts** — Professional judgment: who should use what, damage prevention, salon safety

## Levels / Progression
- Single-game format: all 25 clues in one session
- Progress bar tracks completion percentage across the board
- Final screen assigns a stylist rank based on accuracy:
  - 90%+ : Master Colorist
  - 70-89%: Senior Stylist
  - 50-69%: Junior Colorist
  - 30-49%: Shampoo Apprentice
  - Under 30%: Foil in Training
- Score can go negative if player gets multiple wrong answers (adds realism and stakes)

## Characters
- **Player**: A salon contestant — no avatar required; the board IS the character
- **Mirella** (implied presence): The authoritative voice behind every clue, via her blog content
- **The Clients** (implied): Dark-haired clients waiting for their transformation — the stakes of knowing your bleach!

## Power-Ups / Obstacles
- **Daily Double** (3 tiles): Full-screen retro animation; doubles point value for that clue
- **Skip / Pass**: No penalty, but no reward — strategic use saves a weak score
- **Negative Scoring**: Getting clues wrong at high dollar values can crater your score
- **Blue Pigment Trap** (thematic): The Blonde Solutions category question references how blue formulas obscure lift reading — parallels the challenge of "reading" a tricky clue

## Win / Lose Conditions
- Win: Achieve Master Colorist (90%+ accuracy) or maximize your dollar score
- Lose: End the game with a negative score or the "Foil in Training" rank
- No hard game-over — all 25 questions are always reachable; the rank is the verdict

## Visual Style
- Deep navy and royal blue board (classic game show palette)
- Gold dollar amounts and headers (salon luxury meets arcade flash)
- Teal and pink accents (HBBU brand colors)
- Georgia serif for titles (editorial, beauty editorial feel)

- Green/pink feedback popups float off-screen for correct/wrong answers
- Clean, mobile-responsive layout that scales from phone to desktop

## Arcade Appeal
- Instantly recognizable Jeopardy format = zero learning curve
- Hair-specific content makes it uniquely satisfying for salon pros and beauty enthusiasts
- Daily Double mechanic creates genuine tension and surprise
- Fast to play (5-10 minutes per session) — perfect for arcade cabinet or waiting room tablet
- Competitive: high score and stylist rank create replay motivation
- Educational: players learn real product names, ingredients, and techniques by playing
- Can be updated seasonally as Mirella publishes new ranked guides
