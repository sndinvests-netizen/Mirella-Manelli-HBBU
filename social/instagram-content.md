### Caption

Your hair is talking. Make sure it's saying something worth hearing. 💬

The difference between a good colour and a *great* one? It lives in the tones — and knowing exactly when to go warmer, cooler, or stay right where the light hits you best.

Whether you're in the chair with me or studying the craft yourself, I want you to walk away knowing *why* your hair looks the way it does — not just that it does. ✨

Education is the foundation of every beautiful result.

Save this if you're on a hair journey right now, and drop a comment below — warm or cool tones for you? Let's talk. 👇
